How this project came to life?

I love buying clothes and shoes, but I hate paying the full price for them. I noticed that I spend a lot of time on different websites checking for discounts, instead of coding. I wanted to create a single website, that with a click of a button can search all of the websites I shop from and display the discounted items as they were from a single shop. I achieved this by using Django and WebScrapers. It will surely save me a lot of time and money in the future. This website is not for commercial use! I have not asked for permission any of the websites it scrapes. It is just for personal use and learning purposes.
