How this project came to life?

I love buying clothes and shoes by certain brands, but I hate paying the full price for them. I noticed that I spend a lot of time on different websites checking for discounts, instead of coding. I was currently learning the basics of Django and I also wanted to learn WebScraping. Well if you combine the three things you get this website. When you click Search, the website will search some of the biggest fashion websites in Bulgaria for discounted items that match your needs. This website is not for commercial use! I have not asked for permission any of the websites it scrapes. It is just for personal use and learning purposes.
